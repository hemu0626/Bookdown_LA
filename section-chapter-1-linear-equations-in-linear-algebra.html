1.1.1.1 Linear System (线性系统), Coefficient (系数) and Variable (变量)

A linear equation is \[\begin{equation} a_1x_1+a_2x_2+\cdots+a_nx_n=b \qquad n=1, 2, 3, \cdots, \infty \end{equation}\] \(a_1,\dots,a_n\) are real or complex numbers, called {coefficient}.\ \(x_1,\dots,x_n\) are unknown {variables}\ A system of linear equations is called {linear system}.\
